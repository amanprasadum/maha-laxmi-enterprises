FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our export services to South Africa.

Can I order mixed containers with rice and yarn?

Yes, mixed cargo containers are available based on product compatibility. Our logistics team will help optimize the container space while ensuring product integrity.

Do you provide Halal-certified rice?

Absolutely. We provide Halal-certified rice on request to meet the specific requirements of our South African clients serving Muslim consumers.

How do I know the rice I receive matches what I order?

We provide lab reports, SGS certification, and detailed photos/videos before shipment. This ensures complete transparency and confidence in the quality of products you receive.

Do you help with customs clearance in South Africa?

Yes, we work with experienced agents to assist you with hassle-free clearance. Our team is familiar with South African import regulations and documentation requirements.

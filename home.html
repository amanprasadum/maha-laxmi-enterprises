Frequently Asked Questions

Can I order mixed containers with rice and yarn?
Yes, mixed cargo containers are available based on product compatibility. We can arrange this to optimize your ordering and shipping costs.
Do you provide Halal-certified rice?
Absolutely. We provide Halal-certified rice on request to meet the requirements of diverse markets in South Africa.
How do I know the rice I receive matches what I order?
We provide lab reports, SGS certification, and detailed photos/videos before shipment to ensure complete transparency and quality assurance.
Do you help with customs clearance in South Africa?
Yes, we work with experienced agents to assist you with hassle-free clearance. Our team handles all documentation to ensure smooth import processes.

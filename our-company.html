About Maa Laxmi Enterprises

Leading Indian processors and exporters of premium agro commodities worldwide

Excellence in Agro Commodity Exports

Maa Laxmi Enterprises is one of the leading Indian processors and exporters of various kinds of agro commodities. Our products range from commodities like Basmati and Non-Basmati Rice, Spices, White Refined Sugar and variety of Oil Seeds.

India is one of the largest producers of variety of Basmati Rice, out of which the Northern region offers the best quality in the world. At Maa Laxmi Enterprises, the primary export is of Basmati and non-Basmati Rice from all over the country, which helps our customers get access to the best variety of rice one can possibly get.

Global Reach & Quality Assurance

We have satisfied customers in more than 31 countries including USA, Canada, Australia, Saudi Arabia, UK, European Union, UAE, Kuwait, Qatar, Egypt, Yemen, Oman, South Africa, Turkey and Ukraine.

Our Manufacturing Facilities

State-of-the-art processing facilities ensuring consistent quality and large-scale production

Advanced Processing Infrastructure

With more than 15 rice mills, 8 spice mills and multiple warehouses, our daily production capacity is of 1000 MT of rice each day. Even with such large scale operation we do not compromise with the quality of our rice.

We provide packaging of rice and other products in various sizes ranging from 1kg to 50kg, so as to customize according to the needs of the client. The bags used for packaging includes PP Bags, Non-Woven Bags, BOPP Bags, Jute Bags and a few other types with the buyers logo, label and other details if requested.

Apart from India, our products are easily accessible in USA and Saudi Arabia as we are located in these countries as well. The prospective customers may either reach out to us by contacting our Indian office or any of our International offices.

Our Global Mission

To be the world's most trusted partner in agro commodity exports, connecting the finest Indian produce with global markets.

Connecting India to the World

Dealing with customers from all around the world has helped us gain expertise in analyzing the requirements of buyers worldwide. To help you get the best quality of rice and other products right at your choice of location, we handle all the processes including Processing, Packaging and Exporting.

Our Production Process

From field to export: How we maintain quality at every step

1. Cultivation

Rice is grown in the premium agricultural regions of Northern India using sustainable farming practices.

2. Harvesting

Harvested at optimal maturity to ensure perfect grain development and maximum aroma retention.

3. Aging

Rice is aged for 2 years under controlled conditions to enhance flavor, aroma, and cooking characteristics.

4. Processing

State-of-the-art milling and sorting technology ensures consistent quality and removes impurities.

5. Quality Control

Rigorous testing at our in-house laboratory ensures every batch meets our strict quality standards.

6. Packaging

Vacuum-sealed in premium packaging to maintain freshness and prevent moisture contamination.

7. Export

Carefully handled logistics ensure your order reaches you in perfect condition, anywhere in the world.
